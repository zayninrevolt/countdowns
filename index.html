About the app

Countdowns lets you track the days until anything — a holiday, a birthday, a wedding, a trip — with beautiful backgrounds, Home & Lock Screen widgets, reminders, checklists and locations. Everything stays private to you: no account, no tracking, and optional iCloud sync across your own devices.

  • Home & Lock Screen widgets in every size
  • Theme, colour, gradient or your own photo backgrounds
  • Checklists with progress shown on the widget, plus event locations
  • Reminders and automatic yearly repeats
  • Share by AirDrop, or post a countdown card to social
  • iCloud sync, Import from Calendar, backup & restore
