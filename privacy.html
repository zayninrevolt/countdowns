Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 July 2026

Countdowns is designed to keep your information private. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your data.

The short version

Countdowns does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no servers. Everything you create stays on your device.

What the app stores

Your countdowns — the titles, dates, background choices, and photos you add are saved only on your device (and in your device's own iCloud backup, if you have that turned on in iOS Settings). They are never uploaded to us or anyone else.

Permissions the app may ask for

Photos: only when you choose a photo as a countdown background. The app accesses the specific image you pick; it does not read your wider photo library, and the image never leaves your device except when you personally choose to share a countdown file.

Calendar: only if you use “Import from Calendar.” The app reads your upcoming events so you can pick which to turn into countdowns. It never modifies your calendar and never transmits calendar data anywhere.

Notifications: only if you turn on reminders. Reminders are scheduled locally on your device.

Sharing a countdown

When you share a countdown — by QR code or file — the information you chose to share (such as the title and date) travels directly to the person you send it to, through whatever app or method you pick. It does not pass through any server operated by us.

Children's privacy

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this page with a new “last updated” date.
